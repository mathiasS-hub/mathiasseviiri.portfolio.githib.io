Mathias Seviiri

About

Dr. Mathias Seviiri (MD, MPH) is pursuing a PhD in Statistical Genetics at QIMR Berghofer Medical Research Institute, and Queensland University of Technology, Brisbane, Australia. His work focuses on the genetics of skin cancers especially utilizing polygenic risk scores to profile organ transplant recipients and melanoma patients for skin cancer risk and survival respectively. He has published in top notch journals like Journal of Investigative Dermatology, The Lancet Global Health, Heart (BMJ) among others. His work has been presented at national and international conferences including; the American Society for Human Genetics Conference 2020 and the Australasian Epidemiological Association Conference 2016. He has a rich international educational experience from reputable universities in Australia, Europe and Africa. He previously worked at Cancer Council Victoria, and with the Ministry of Health, in Uganda. His research interests include;cancer genetics and epidemiology, and precision health.
